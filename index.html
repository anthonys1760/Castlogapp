HOW IT HAPPENED

The Fishbrain betrayal,
year by year.

2018
SoftBank money enters the picture
Fishbrain raises $13.5M from B Capital & SoftBank. The app shifts from "community for anglers" to "product that needs to return VC money."
March 2021
$31M raise — paywalls begin immediately
Features users already had — statistics, private posts, catch zoom — get locked behind subscriptions. Not new features. Existing ones.
December 2021
Fish Rules acquired. Aspira partnership signed.
Fishbrain acquires Fish Rules and partners with Aspira — North America's largest fishing license software provider. Anglers are now a data pipeline.
November 2023
Fishbrain acquired by Aspira. Mission over.
The app built "for anglers" is owned by a B2B government licensing company in Dallas whose core business is selling hunting and fishing permits to state agencies.
